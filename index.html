Rain Bounty!  

Harvest the rains reap the gains!!



RainBounty will help you get started with rainwater harvesting and optimize your harvesting gains.


The RainBounty App uses historical data and future forecasts to provide you with essential information when deciding the configuration of your rainwater harvesting system. The app also serves as a resource for rainwater harvesters with its Learn More and Frequently Asked Questions sections.
